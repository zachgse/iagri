I-Agri is developed by 4th year students from De La Salle-College of Saint Benilde Manila. I-Agri is a platform that help companies and organizations associated with the agricultural sector in the Philippines in storing, maintaining, and keeping their personal records in a secure and organize way. Ranging to different business processes and functions such as trade, importation, exportation, and the need for supplies of the products including fresh animal produce, vegetables and fruits supplied to various local supermarkets and restaurants.


What do we do?

Collect and present logs of transactions

Mini Inventory System


Automate farm processes

Dashboard about activities and updates


Present Information and records


Serves as a platform for potential partnerships


Personal page of partners


Vision, Mission & Goal

Vision

To create useful features and functionalities for farmers that informs and eases their processes and activities

Mission

To enable farm businesses easily adapt and connect to the digital world across the nation

Goal

To be a leading platform for farm businesses in maintaining and securing their records

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

To register an account, go to the IAGRI homepage and click on register on the top right corner of the system. Once clicking register, it will redirect you to the registration page. Fill out the required information and select the type of user that would like to register as. Once completing the form, click on sign up, then you will be redirected to the IAGRI homepage. Note: Only farm businesses that are willing to make partnerships with our platform are allowed to register an account.

The platform requires the user to pay a Php 1,000 pesos fee before being able to utilize the use of the platform. From having a social media presence through the platform, to the utilization of the features and functionalities of the system such as digitized and automated reports and sheets and the mini inventory system inside the platform.

To edit the company profile, go to the profile page by clicking the "profile" button on the top right corner of the system. Then click the "profile" page on the leftmost part of the system. Lastly, fill out the necessary information and click the "save" button. However, it only applies to active users who were verified by the System Administrators.

The system allows the users to input products, animals and transactions which will reflect the current stocks and the total sales acquired from the listed products of the user. The system also allows the users to freely add stocks, edit, and update all the necessary information regarding to their needs.
