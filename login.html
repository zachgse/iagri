I-Agri

I-Agri is developed by 4th year students from De La Salle-College of Saint Benilde Manila. I-Agri is a platform that help companies and organizations associated with the agricultural sector in the Philippines in storing, maintaining, and keeping their personal records in a secure and organize way. Ranging to different business processes and functions such as trade, importation, exportation, and the need for supplies of the products including fresh animal produce, vegetables and fruits supplied to various local supermarkets and restaurants.
